<p><i>QuotesApp</i> lets you read and save quotes. It helps you stay motivated by providing best picked quotes stated by famous personalities. This app uses <a href='https://zenquotes.io/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>zen quotes</a> API for retrieving quotes.</p><p>This App has useful features, such as bookmarks, to save the quotes that you find motivating, and keep them in a separated section. Moreover, there are a number of cool transition and animation affects, which makes navigation much cooler. Long pressing a quote in your Bookmarks copies that quote to the clipboard... More features along the way.</p>
