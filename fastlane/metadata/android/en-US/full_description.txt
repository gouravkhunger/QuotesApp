<p><b>QuotesApp</b> lets you read and save quotes. It helps you stay motivated by providing best picked quotes stated by famous personalities. This app uses <a href='https://zenquotes.io/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>zen quotes</a> API for retrieving quotes.</p><p>It has useful features, such as daily dose of wisdom through a notification, bookmarks to save the quotes that you find motivating and keeping them in a separated section as well as capturing beautiful images of the quotes with the share button.</p><p>Moreover, there are a number of cool transition and animation affects, like slide to refresh, which makes navigation much cooler. Long pressing a quote in your Bookmarks copies it to the clipboard... More features along the way.</p>
